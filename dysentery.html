Diarrhoea

Home Remedies:

  • 1-2 gm. powder of fried cumin seeds with 250 ml. butter milk four times daily.
  • Ash of the cardamom skin 2 gm. with a little honey 4-5 times a day.
  • 10 ml. Fluid of pomegranate skin thrice a day. Fruit may be eaten in plenty.
  • Diarrhoea in children:
    Pinch of powder of Jayphal or fruit (Jayphal) rubbed on a clean surface with milk or water should be given 3-4 times a day.
