Cough

Home Remedies:

  • Warm liquid of 2 gm. Cumin(jeera), 5 gm. Coriander(dhania), 1 gm. Turmeric(haldi), 1 gm. Fenugreek(methi) powder and little pepper should be taken with honey/ sugar with lemon two to three times.
  • Chewing few grains of cumin frequently helps in dry as well as productive cough.
  • Little cardamom(Elaichi) powder with a tea spoon full of honey 3-4 times day.
  • Chewing of cinnamon controls the throat irritation and helps in dry cough.
  • Chewing cardamom (Not more than 3 per day) frequently is helpful in dry as well as productive cough.
